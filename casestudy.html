Catch Bus
Reliable Aid for Public
Bus Transport of Punjab

The Challenge

People in Punjab heavily depend on public transport such as buses but are not happy with the service. If they miss their bus they have to wait for a long time to catch another. Moreover in such a busy schedule, they can’t afford to wait for so long hopelessly.


The Solution

Our main goal is people can easily track their bus and reach their destination without any hassle. People can also know how crowded their bus is, which can alert those who cannot stand and travel. We offer various other features for a stress-free journey.


The Process followed

User Flow

User Flow reflects the user’s progress from the launch of the application to the discovery of all functionalities in the app and doing some actions.


Concept Sketching

They are typically concept sketches, a set of drawings or storyboard, representing the skeleton of the interface.


Medium Fidelity Wireframes

Onboarding

We wanted our users to know all the features they can expect in the application in the onboarding process.

Easy sign Up process

An opt out option, keeping in mind most of the users are in a rush while looking for a bus, and barely have time to sign in at the moment.

Home and Key features

The plan your trip feature allows you to instantly plan and schedule trips also gives you an option of paying online right when you schedule the trip.

Online Payment

Online payment can be done via different popular payment methods including cards, UPI and even the own Catch Bus Wallet

Online Tickets

Generating an online ticket, which solves the need to carry your paper ticket, also keeping a record of all the previously bought tickets.

Reminders and Rewards

A reminder before the arrival of his bus so that one can reach the bus stop on time. We also offer rewards on the number of times you purchase a ticket from Catch Bus.

Catch Bus : App All Screens

Conclusion

We designed a seamless solution to reduce the big gap between commuters and bus service. We learnt form this project that research plays an important role in the output of the design and simple designs are much more preferred.
